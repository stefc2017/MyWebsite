Degree Planner Home Page

Android app I made in my class of software engineering with my group. There is a presentation layer, business logic layer and a persistence layer.

Degree Planner Degrees Page

My group and I used Android Studio as our IDE and HSQLDB as our database as well as Robotium for acceptance testing and Git for source control.

Degree Planner Degree Information

This page displays the degree, how many credits the degree requires and the list of courses the student needs to take in order to complete their degree.

Degree Planner Add Course

This page lets the student plan their future school terms. The student can select the term and the year they are planning to take the selected course.

Degree Planner Course Planning

The student can view the courses they are planning to take as well as add or remove a course from their term schedule or even move a course to another term.

Degree Planner Degree Progress

The student can choose their degree and see how many credit hours they have taken and the courses they are eligible to take based on courses they have taken.
