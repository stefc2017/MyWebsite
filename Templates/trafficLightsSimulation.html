Green light and cross

Traffic Lights Simulation made in Java using Swing and the graphics library.

Source

Green light and stop

The traffic light will start with green light and crossing sign but at 5 seconds left, the hand to tell the pedestrians to stop will show.

Yellow light and stop

The yellow light will show when the crosswalk hits 3 seconds left to warn the drivers to stop.

Red light and stop

The red light will show when the crosswalk hits 0 seconds and will be red for 20 seconds until the crosswalk hits 0 again and then the simulation will restart.
